Glossary

But there's more that makes glossaries interesting. WE AUTHOR IN PARALLEL TO PRODUCT DEV. As Kasia Kolodziejczak said yesterday, tis is one of the hardest things to do. So we may develop our doc according to prod milestones, but internally, in TechPubs we also have paralel project lifecycles. one of them is our reused glossary. gloss milestones =/= doc milestones. You may know you have a term, and you want to write content, but the tterm will not be added the gloss immediately. Gloos update can take from days to weeks to months, depending on the particular situation of a given company. (it has to be approved by x smes, by marketing, synch with other toolds...) It may also be that you know what you need to document before your stakeholdesr (PO, marketing) have cristalised naming conventions -- in that case, it is obvious that you need to wait before you can propose a new term.
